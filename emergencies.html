Emergency Procedures

React fast. Stay calm. Prioritize safety.

Step 1: Mechanical Failure

If a kart is smoking, leaking, or loses power:

  • Signal with a red or black flag.
  • Radio marshals to slow and safely stop the session.
  • Direct driver off track to pit lane if possible.
  • Never allow guests to push or investigate karts themselves.

Step 2: Medical Emergency

If someone is injured:

  • Immediately stop the session ‚Äî use red flags.
  • Only trained staff provide first aid.
  • Contact supervisor and call 000 if serious.
  • Clear spectators and ensure safe perimeter.
  • Log incident in the official incident book after it's resolved.

Step 3: Fire or Hazard

In case of fire, fuel leak, or electrical danger:

  • Evacuate the area.
  • Use extinguishers ONLY if safe and trained to do so.
  • Turn off all power if electrical.
  • Alert supervisor and emergency services.
  • Do not re-enter until cleared by manager or fire crew.

Step 4: Evacuation

If ordered to evacuate:

  • Guide guests to emergency exits or assembly points.
  • Stay calm, speak clearly, and keep groups together.
  • Do NOT run unless life is in immediate danger.
  • Never leave guests alone or allow re-entry.
