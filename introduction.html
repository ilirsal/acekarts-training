Introduction

Welcome to Ace Karts Training

Step 1: Who We Are

Ace Karts is one of Australia’s most exciting outdoor karting venues, known for fast, safe, and adrenaline-filled racing. We’ve been operating for over 20 years and pride ourselves on delivering unforgettable motorsport experiences to people of all ages and skill levels.

Step 2: Our Mission & Values

Our mission is simple: Deliver the safest, most fun, and professional karting experience in the country. We value:

  • 🛡️ Safety – Our #1 priority
  • 🏁 Excellence – Professionalism and quality in everything
  • 😊 Customer Experience – Every guest should feel welcomed and thrilled
  • 🤝 Teamwork – We work together to make each day smooth and safe

Step 3: Your Role as Staff

As a staff member, you are the face of Ace Karts. You're responsible for safety, fun, and making sure our guests leave smiling. This includes:

  • ✅ Greeting customers professionally
  • ✅ Ensuring all safety procedures are followed
  • ✅ Knowing how to handle basic track operations
  • ✅ Communicating clearly and positively with teammates

Step 4: What to Expect in This Training

This training will take you through every part of working at Ace Karts, including:

  • 🕓 Pre-opening checks
  • 🚥 Track operations and safety
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Interacting with customers
  • 📢 Emergency procedures
  • 🎯 Your responsibilities as part of the team

Everything is broken down step-by-step, so don’t worry — just take it one screen at a time.
