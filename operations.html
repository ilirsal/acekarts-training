Daily Operations

Keep the race day running smoothly.

Step 1: Bookings & Schedule

Start by checking the daily schedule:

  • Use the booking software (e.g., TryBooking, Eventbrite).
  • Confirm group sizes, arrival times, and session types.
  • Note special requirements: exclusive track hire, birthday packages, etc.

Step 2: Session Flow

Each session follows a structure:

  • Briefing ‚Üí Gear-up ‚Üí Kart allocation ‚Üí On-track session ‚Üí Debrief.
  • Time each part. Don‚Äôt allow delays to stack up.
  • Communicate with staff via radio to stay in sync.

Step 3: Kart Rotation

Rotate karts to avoid overheating and balance usage:

  • Use a rotation chart or whiteboard in pit area.
  • If a kart is running rough or frequently used, rotate it out.
  • Report any faults immediately to supervisor.

Step 4: Communication

Radio comms are crucial:

  • All staff must wear radios.
  • Keep messages clear and short.
  • Examples: "Group A karts out", "Red flag turn 3", "Session 4 ready".
