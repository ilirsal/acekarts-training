Staff Conduct

Professionalism on and off the track

Step 1: Personal Presentation

First impressions matter. Your appearance reflects our brand:

  • Always wear the Ace Karts uniform, clean and neat.
  • Closed-toe shoes are mandatory. Steel-cap preferred in pit areas.
  • No excessive jewelry or distracting accessories.
  • Maintain good hygiene and grooming.

Step 2: Communication

Speak clearly, politely, and respectfully:

  • Use positive language with customers, even in stressful situations.
  • Address concerns calmly and professionally.
  • Use radios correctly: no swearing or chatter during sessions.
  • Speak to team members with the same respect you expect.

Step 3: Teamwork & Attitude

We operate as one team. Your attitude impacts the day:

  • Support your teammates, even outside your usual role.
  • Take initiative ‚Äî don't wait to be asked to help.
  • Leave personal issues at the gate ‚Äî focus on the guest experience.
  • Keep things fun, but safe and professional.
